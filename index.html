Features

Everything your event needs

From intimate gatherings to neighborhood block parties, Populr gives you the tools to host, discover, and connect with ease.

📅
Smart Event Creation
Create beautiful event pages in seconds. Add details, photos, location, external ticketing, and RSVP flows — all in one place.
🗺️
Local Discovery
Find what's happening near you. Browse events on a live map or personalized feed curated to your interests and neighborhood.
💬
Community Connection
Chat with attendees, share updates, coordinate carpools, and build genuine connections around shared experiences.
🎟️
Easy RSVPs
Free events, waitlists, and guest management — handled beautifully. No third-party apps or clunky links required.
🔔
Smart Reminders
Never miss a moment. Populr sends timely reminders and real-time updates so your attendees show up excited and on time.
📸
Shared Memories
Capture and share photos in a collaborative event album. Every attendee contributes — you get one beautiful place to relive it all.
How It Works

Simple as 1, 2, 3, 4

From idea to celebration in four easy steps.

1
Create Your Event
Add the details — name, time, place, vibe. Publish in under a minute.
2
Share & Invite
Send invites directly, share your event link, or let locals discover it on the map.
3
Build the Buzz
Chat with your attendees, post updates, and watch the excitement build before the big day.
4
Celebrate Together
Enjoy the event, capture shared memories, and keep the community alive long after.
